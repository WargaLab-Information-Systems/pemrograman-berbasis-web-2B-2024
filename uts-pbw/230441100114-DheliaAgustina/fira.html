Sinopsis

Chainsaw Man (Japanese: チェンソーマン, Hepburn: Chensō Man) is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since December 2018, and has been serialized in seventeen tankōbon volumes as of April 2024.

next→

Denji

Denji, is the main protagonist of the series Chainsaw man. As a young boy, he inherits his father's debts from the Yakuza. After meeting Pochita, he becomes a Devil Hunter for the Yakuza in an attempt to clear his debt. Once the Yakuza, under the control of a Devil, betrays him, he dies and Pochita becomes his heart. He is then revived and can become the Chainsaw Man by pulling a cord on his chest. After meeting Makima, he became a Public Safety Devil Hunter as a member of Tokyo Special Division 4.

Power

Power possesses the corpse of an unknown woman or girl with long hair. She mainly appears being blonde. Her hair color varies from blonde to pink in manga illustrations, even appearing dirty blonde in some official art, and appears strawberry blonde in the anime. As a Fiend, she has red and yellow eyes that form a cross pattern, sharp teeth with defined fangs, and light red horns protruding from the top of her head. If Power accumulates too much blood, her horns become larger and more curved.

Hayakawa Aki

Aki Hayakawa was the deuteragonist of the series Chainsaw Man. Aki was a young man with medium-length black hair that he usually wore in a topknot style (shoulder-length with long bangs when untied) and blue eyes. He was a Public Safety Devil Hunter working under Makima's special squad who had contracts with the Curse Devil and Future Devil, and a former contract with the Fox Devil. During the Gun Devil arc, he was killed and possessed by 20% of the Gun Devil, thus becoming the host of the Gun Fiend.

“ Denji has a simple dream—to live happily and peacefully, to spend time with the girl he likes. However, this is far from reality, as Denji is forced by the yakuza to kill demons to pay off his huge debts. Using his pet demon Pochita as a weapon, he is ready to do anything with little money. Unfortunately, he has outlived his usefulness and was killed by a demon under contract with the yakuza. However, in an unexpected turn of events, Pochita merges with Denji's corpse and gives him the demonic power of a chainsaw. Now able to turn parts of his body into chainsaws, the revived Denji uses his new abilities to quickly and brutally defeat his enemies. Looking at the eyes of the official demon hunters who arrived at the scene, he was offered a job in the Public Security Bureau as one of them. Now with the means to take on even the toughest enemies, Denji will stop at nothing to achieve his humble teenage dream."
